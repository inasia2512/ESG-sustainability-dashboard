# ESG-sustainability-dashboard
The dashboard transforms sustainability data into actionable business intelligence through executive KPI tracking, trend analysis, industry benchmarking, and geospatial insights. Designed with a modern enterprise-style ESG theme, the project focuses on connecting environmental responsibility with financial and operational performance.

Key Features :- 
1.ESG score trend analysis
2.Carbon emission benchmarking by industry
3.Renewable energy usage insights
4.CSR spending analytics
5.Female workforce representation analysis
6.Country-wise water usage visualization
7.Sustainability rating distribution
8.Interactive filters and dynamic visual storytelling

Project Objective:-
To demonstrate how sustainability and environmental metrics can be integrated with business analytics for smarter ESG-driven decision making and corporate intelligence.

Tools & Technologies:-
1.Power BI
2.DAX
3.Data Modeling
4.Data Visualization
5.ESG Analytics
6.Business Intelligence
